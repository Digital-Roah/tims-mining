Mineral Quality Control

Analytical data errors can lead to erroneous resource estimate. Timadix addresses this area critically and has a dedicated team to interact with the analytical lab. We ensure batch wise real-time control gate analysis and quality assurance program. Timadix has an in-depth knowledge of the principles of Quality Control and the expertise in designing Quality Control programmes to achieve best practices in data quality. We apply modern sampling theories for sample size selection and design sampling protocols. Our Quality Control protocols ensure highest data quality standards and best practices to ensure data is fit for purpose.

Some of these services include :

  • Design and implementation of Quality Control protocol.
  • Batch wise control gate analysis with statistical tools to identify issues related to accuracy, precision and bias in analytical results.
  • Besides analytical data, Timadix also conducts Quality Control on topography survey, collar and down hole survey and any data which can impact accuracy of resource estimates.
  • Design and implementation of logging and sampling protocol.
  • Monitoring chain of custody of samples.
  • Creation and maintaining Quality Control online database with lab and project team integrated on common platform to effectively monitor sample turn around and to reduce human errors.
  • Independent audit of 3rd party analytical data.

About Us

TIMADIX GEOMIN CONSULT NIG LTD, Founded In 2017 is a geological / Mining Engineering Firm with headquarters in Abuja, FCT, Nigeria, the company majors in Geological Prospecting of mineral Resources, core drilling, mineral appreciation and beneficiation, and mineral dealership with mining leases scattered accross the middle belt region of Nigeria, TGC is involved in the mining of Lithium, Gold, Lead, Zinc and other solid minerals with over 10years of experience. There are other prospects of Lithium mining but actual mining of said mineral is yet to commence.

Mining Activities

Timadix Geomin Consult Nig ltd, or TGC as it is commonly called, is a Geotechnical and mining consultancy firm which also practices mining and mineral separation. with the mother beneficiation plant in Jos, Plateau state, We are able to beneficiate up to 8 metallic minerals. We are dedicated to augmenting ores within the ambits of our separation equipment before exporting them or selling locally.

About Us

TIMADIX GEOMIN CONSULT NIG LTD, Founded In 2017 is a geological / Mining Engineering Firm with headquarters in Jos, Plateau state, the company majors in Geological Prospecting of mineral Resources, core drilling, mineral appreciation and beneficiation, and mineral dealership with mining leases scattered accross the middle belt region of Nigeria, TGC is involved in the mining of Tin, Tantalum, Lead, zinc and Gold. There are other prospects of Lithium mining but actual mining of said mineral is yet to commence.

We boast of robust prospecting tools with some of the best prospecting machines in the country such as the U33 drone and other high resolution magnetometers.

We stand a good chance of locating most metallic deposits

The Managing Director

Engr Tim Eldon is the Managing Director of Timadix Geomin Consult Nig Ltd. He has a BSc in Geology and Mining Engineering from the University of Jos with over 8years of experience. He has personally worked on over eight projects both as a supervisor and as a team player. These experiences have made him well suited for consultation on standard procedures. He has worked in various countries in Africa including Nigeria, Cameroon , Central African republic and Tanzania.

He has the passion to unveil mining in Africa to both Indigenous and foreign investor and making it a profitable enterprise rather than the unstable and risky business as it is predominantly thought to be.
